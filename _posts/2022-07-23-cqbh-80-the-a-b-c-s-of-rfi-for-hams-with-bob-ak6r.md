<p>On this CQ Blind Hams Tech Zoom. Bob Brehm AK6R gives us the ABC's of RFI for hams. &nbsp;&nbsp;Bob is Currently Chief Engineer at www.Palomar-Engineers.com - &nbsp;</p>
